Students Grades 6 - 12: Enter the UPN Mural Contest!
Submit by 3:00 PM on Sunday, March 26.

The Upcounty Prevention Network (UPN) in collaboration with community partner Riverworks Art Center is organizing a contest for creative students in the Poolesville, Maryland area. The object is to design a mural to be located in downtown Poolesville with the theme Mindful of the Past, Hopeful for the Future. The winning design will be painted with collaboration from student and adult artists and will be unveiled this spring!
